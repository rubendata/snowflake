Playbook

How I'll Drive Automotive Growth in EMEA

I have a clear, repeatable process to accelerate Snowflake's automotive growth across EMEA. The Field CTO Flywheel delivers clear outcomes at each phase through systematic collaboration with Sales, SEs, and SI partners. This approach scales: what works gets packaged, trained, and executed by the field independently.

The Field CTO Flywheel

One Cycle
60-180 days
Understand
Discover patterns, validate plays, prioritize opportunities
Create
Build Solution Accelerators with reference architectures and SI packages
Enable
Train field, launch thought leadership, activate partners
Win
Field executes autonomously, playbook improves with every cycle

UNDERSTAND Phase

What I Do What You Get
1. Sales & Opportunity Alignment
  • Join GTM leadership meetings to review customer status and pipeline
  • Prioritize: expand consumption (existing), engage top prospects, win pilots/PoCs
  • Define where FCTO adds value vs field handles autonomously
 Prioritized account list with engagement criteria
2. Customer Discovery & Product Feedback
  • Shadow 15-20 calls across deal stages: stuck deals, early-stage, successful opportunities
  • Create diagnosis report: recurring blockers (technical, commercial, perception), success patterns, EMEA patterns (SAP/non-SAP, multi-cloud, Tier-1 sharing, regulatory)
  • Consolidate product gaps with business impact and competitive context: top features with € pipeline at risk
 Field discovery playbook + prioritized product feedback with business case
3. FCTO Community & Play Validation
  • Learn from global FCTO peers
  • Analyze win/loss (Databricks, AWS, Snowflake)
  • Validate 4 plays: Smart Factory, SDV/ADAS, Supply Chain, Connected Vehicle
 Play strategy confirmed for EMEA

CREATE Phase

What I Do What You Get
1. Build Solution Accelerators for Validated Plays
  • Reference architecture (Snowflake features, data sources, architecture diagram)
  • Deployment guide (week-by-week: discovery → setup → demo → handoff)
  • Demo/template (sample dashboards, example queries, demo data)
  • SI delivery package (SOW template, pricing model, timeline)
  • Examples: Smart Factory (OEE analytics), Supply Chain (control tower), Connected Vehicle (telemetry analysis)
 Field can propose pilot in first customer meeting with pre-built kit
2. Create Enablement Assets
  • Battlecards (e.g., "Why Snowflake vs SAP BW for non-SAP data integration")
  • Discovery question frameworks (e.g., "What's your Azure vs AWS split?")
 SEs know how to qualify and compete

ENABLE Phase

What I Do What You Get
1. Train Field on Automotive Business
  • Monthly sessions on automotive fundamentals: business model dynamics, industry transformation (SDV, electrification), competitive pressures
  • Deal clinics for stuck accounts (office hours format)
  • Hands-on training: how to demo Solution Accelerators, how to scope pilots
 New SEs ramp in 2 weeks, can execute deals independently
2. Build Reference Story Bank
  • Map existing use cases to automotive (e.g., NYSE processes 1.2 trillion order messages/day → automotive telemetry at comparable scale)
  • Curate customer stories (e.g., Nissan validates ProPILOT autonomous driving using billions of sensor data points on Snowflake)
  • EMEA-specific examples where relevant (Catena-X, GDPR, TISAX)
 Proven patterns and customer references for every conversation
3. Thought Leadership & Demand Generation
  • Customer webinars (positioning Snowflake for automotive transformation)
  • Industry conferences (2-3 events: IAA Mobility, automotive data summits)
  • AWS/partner joint webinars (co-marketing with cloud/SI partners)
 Inbound pipeline, industry credibility, field has customer references

WIN Phase

What I Do What You Get
1. Scale Through Field & Partners
  • Field executes autonomously (SEs close 80% without FCTO)
  • SIs deliver pilots at scale (e.g., Reply implements 5 Smart Factory pilots in parallel)
 FCTO only needed for strategic escalations, delivery never a blocker
2. Continuous Improvement Loop
  • Capture wins/losses, identify next blockers
  • Refine plays, update assets, activate new partners
 Playbook improves with every cycle, loops back to UNDERSTAND phase
