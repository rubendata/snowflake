Playbook

How I'll Drive Automotive Growth in EMEA

A repeatable process to accelerate Snowflake's automotive growth in EMEA. The Flywheel delivers outcomes through collaboration with Sales, SEs, and SI partners. What works gets packaged and executed by the field independently.

The Field CTO Flywheel

One Cycle
60-180 days
Understand
Discover patterns, validate plays, prioritize opportunities
Create
Build Solution Accelerators with reference architectures and SI packages
Enable
Train field, launch thought leadership, activate partners
Win
Field executes autonomously, playbook improves with every cycle

UNDERSTAND Phase

What I Do What You Get
Sales & Opportunity Alignment: Join GTM meetings, prioritize accounts, define FCTO engagement criteria Prioritized account list
Customer Discovery: Shadow 15-20 calls, create diagnosis report (blockers, success patterns, EMEA-specific) Field discovery playbook + product feedback
Play Validation: Learn from FCTO peers, analyze win/loss, validate plays (Smart Factory, Supply Chain, Connected Vehicle) Play strategy confirmed for EMEA

CREATE Phase

What I Do What You Get
Solution Accelerators: Reference architecture, deployment guide, demo/template, SI package (Smart Factory, Supply Chain, Connected Vehicle) Field proposes pilots with pre-built kit
Enablement Assets: Battlecards (Snowflake vs SAP BW), discovery frameworks, competitive positioning SEs know how to qualify and compete

ENABLE Phase

What I Do What You Get
Field Training: Monthly automotive sessions, deal clinics, hands-on Solution Accelerator demos New SEs ramp in 2 weeks
Reference Story Bank: Map Snowflake wins to automotive (Nissan, NYSE scale), EMEA context (Catena-X, GDPR) Proven patterns for every conversation
Thought Leadership: Customer webinars, conferences (IAA Mobility), partner co-marketing Inbound pipeline, industry credibility

WIN Phase

What I Do What You Get
Scale Through Partners: Field executes autonomously, SIs deliver pilots at scale FCTO only for strategic escalations
Continuous Improvement: Capture wins/losses, refine plays, activate new partners Playbook improves every cycle
